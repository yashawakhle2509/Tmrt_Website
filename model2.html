PROTON

The smooth, stable, and highly maneuverable ROV is comprised of six thrusters, a rugged frame, and quick-swappable batteries. Powerful but dimmable lights provide excellent illumination for the live HD video feed.

Specifications Vehicle Details
DoF 6(Roll,Pitch,Yaw,Surge,Sway,Heave)
Weight 11-12kgs(tentative)
Dimensions 457mm x 338mm x 254mm
Endurance 150 Minutes
Depth Rating 328ft
Pressure Vessels One for the electronics and one for the batteries. The pressure vessels are designed to protect the sensitive electronics and batteries from water ingress and high-pressure environments while allowing for efficient cooling and heat dissipation.
Actuation System The actuation system of the Proton includes six thrusters that provide precise control of the vehicle's movement and orientation, as well as a gripper arm for manipulating objects. The thrusters are controlled by a custom electronic speed controller that communicates with the vehicle's onboard computer for real-time control and feedback.
Power Lithium Polymer Batteries
Feedback Control Attitude Control, Depth Control, Heading Control, OpenROV Cockpit
